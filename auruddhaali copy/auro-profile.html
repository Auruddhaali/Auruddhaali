About Me

My name is Auruddha Rushd Ali, a dedicated programmer and creative developer hailing from Bangladesh. My passion for technology began at a young age, leading me to explore various programming languages and tools. I specialize in web development, crafting modern, interactive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. For fun, I design engaging games on Scratch, where I've created adventure games, puzzles, and educational projects that challenge logic and creativity. Beyond coding, I enjoy staying updated with the latest tech trends, contributing to open-source projects on GitHub, and connecting with fellow developers worldwide. I'm always excited about new opportunities to innovate, collaborate, and bring ideas to life through code. Whether it's optimizing user interfaces with glassmorphism effects like those on this site or developing full-fledged applications, I'm committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible. If you're interested in web technologies or game design, I'd love to connect!
